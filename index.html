ABOUT US

This club is a place where students find the latest information about topics related to the Internet of Things. Students of the IOTRONICS club will be offered various programs and activities such as seminars, workshops, short courses, and many as listed below. The club is supported by internal and external experts who assist the students in strategizing and organizing activities to help them gain knowledge and skills related to lot. The club aims to guide students and prepare them to pursue their professional careers in industries specializing based on loT.
